Post Test

1. Which of the following networks use circuit switching?

a) the internet
b) telephone networks
c) satellite network
d) wireless sensor networks

2. Which one is not a step in circuit switching?

a) making the connection
b) sending data
c) ending the connection
d) breaking data into packets

3. What is the main problem with circuit switching?

a) it is very slow
b) it loses data
c) wastes resources if no data is sent
d) bad sound quality

4. If the connection break in circuit switching, what happens?

a) call ends
b) call is saved
c) call continues
d) automatically reconnects

5. What happens during the "Circuit Disconnection" phase in Circuit Switching?

a) Data is compressed
b) A new circuit is created
c) Packets are routed through dynamic paths
d) All intermediary links between sender and receiver are terminated
