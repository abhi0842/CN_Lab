Network Topology : Hybrid


To perform the simulation, follow the below given steps:
1. Click on either PC or laptop (present at the bottom) to place the device on the working area.
2. Now click on either switch or hub.
3. Now connect the devices to create a ring topology.
NOTE: Click a device port and drag and drop it to another device port to connect.
4. Click on the check button to verify if the constructed topology is built correctly or not.
5. Again click on either PC or laptop to make other topology.
6. Now click on either switch or hub.
7. Now connect the devices to create a star topology.


Fig. 1 Hybrid topology (click on the image)

8. Click on the check button to verify.
9. Now connect the two topologies to make them hybrid.
10. Click on the check button to verify.
